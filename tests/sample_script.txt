EXT. VENICE BEACH BOARDWALK - SUNSET

A drone rises slowly over the boardwalk, capturing the golden hour light. Below, JOGGERS, SKATERS, and STREET PERFORMERS fill the frame. The camera pushes in on MAYA (28), a determined young filmmaker, who walks purposefully through the crowd carrying a vintage 16mm camera.

MAYA (V.O.)
Every story starts somewhere. Mine started here.

50 extras populate the boardwalk as background performers.

EXT. SPRING STREET, DOWNTOWN LA - NIGHT

Rain slicks the pavement. Neon signs reflect in puddles. DETECTIVE CRUZ (40s) walks alone, collar up, scanning doorways. She spots a SHADOWY FIGURE ducking into an alley.

A POLICE CRUISER screeches to a halt at the corner. Two OFFICERS jump out.

OFFICER MARTINEZ
Cruz! We got a visual on the suspect — north alley!

Cruz draws her service weapon and moves toward the alley. 20 extras mill around as bystanders, some ducking for cover.

INT. ABANDONED WAREHOUSE - NIGHT

Smoke drifts through broken windows. MARCUS (30s) sits at a metal table. He pulls a PISTOL from his waistband and slides it across the table.

The RIVAL GANG LEADER picks up a RIFLE leaning against the wall. Eight actors face off in tense silence.

MARCUS
You want the territory? Take it. But you leave my people alone.

EXT. GRIFFITH PARK - DAY

Maya and JORDAN (30) sit on a bench overlooking the city skyline. Three GOLDEN RETRIEVERS trot past with a dog walker. Birds chirp. A peaceful contrast to the previous scenes.

JORDAN
You can't keep doing this. The film isn't worth your life.

MAYA
It's not about the film anymore. It's about the truth.
